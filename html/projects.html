Backyard Transformation
This project took place on the outskirts of the city. The home owner recently moved into this new house and inherited a rock, weed, and debris infested area of land, that was the perfect size for a garden. Over the course of 3 weeks, this garden was prepared and planted, to grow pumpkins, beans, peas, and cucumbers. The greatest struggle of this project was the multitude of rocks, mixed in with tall grasses.
