Aviela's ideal future career would be holding an Art Director position, or owning her own design firm. According to Wikipedia, an "Art director is the title for a variety of similar job functions in theater, advertising, marketing, publishing, fashion, film and television, the Internet, and video games. It is the charge of a sole art director to supervise and unify the vision of an artistic production." When asked where she would like to live the most, Aviela responded, "Austria".
